Here Is What You Need To Know ?

1. Standard competitive bomb plant scenario. 2. 30 rounds per match, the team to win first 16 matches wins. 3. Overtime will be issued in case of a tie.

All competitive CS rules apply –

  1. Friendly fire – ON
  2. Armor Purchasable
  3. Start Money - $800
  4. Defuse kit Purchasable
    Map Pool–
  1. de_dust2
  2. de_mirage
  3. de_cache

Registered teams will play in the following bracket:

Group stage -
Four teams per group. Total of 6 games to decide winner of each group Let A, B, C, D be the teams. Then the games would be -
1. A vs B
2. C vs D
3. Winner of 1 vs Winner of 2
4. Loser of 1 vs Loser of 2
5. Loser of 3 vs Winner of 4
6. Winner of 3 vs Winner of 5
Winner of 6 advances to Playoffs

The Game would be played as best of 1 map (de_dust2) in group stage.

Playoffs -

Single match elimination/Knock out system.
The Game would be played as best of 3 map (9 rounds each of Map 1, 2, 3) in the playoffs.
