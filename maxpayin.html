The Race Against Time


Here Is What You Need To Know ?


Overview :


Event consists of 4 different levels, the difficulty level increasing gradually after each level. Participants have to solve a variety of coding questions according to a particular theme and level. Prize will be given to participants level wise.

The event will be conducted on 10th and 11th of March from 11:00 AM to 03:00 PM at Computer Centre, SGSITS.


Detailed Summary:


There will be three different channels to enter the competition.
1. Shoot a target for shooters
2. Solve a riddle
3. Win a game for gamers


After completing any one of the above activity .


Level 1:

Objective type mathematical and logical random questions. Time constraints 5-10 min. Prize only coupons if he/she want to quit otherwise go to next level.

Level 2:

One Simple coding question. Time constraint 20 min. Fixed prize will be given to winner or he/she will get prize of this category by shooting and then quit the game . If he/she want to go for the next round then prize of current level will not be given to him/her

Level 3:

One Intermediate coding question. Time constraint 20 min. Fixed prize will be given to winner or he/she will get prize of this category by shooting and then quit the game . If he/she want to go for the next round then prize of current level will not be given to him/her

Final round:

Winners of Level 2 will compete in this round. This round will be organised at codechef. Winners will get harddisk etc.

Team which opt for prizes will have to shoot on the prize and get the prize.


Eligibility criterion and participation:


A person will be eligible to play the game only once. His mobile number will be verified.
