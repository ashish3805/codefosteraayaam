Registered players will play in the following bracket:

Group stage -
Four players per group. Total of 6 games to decide winner of each group Let A, B, C, D be the players. Then the games would be -
1. A vs B
2. C vs D
3. Winner of 1 vs Winner of 2
4. Loser of 1 vs Loser of 2
5. Loser of 3 vs Winner of 4
6. Winner of 3 vs Winner of 5
Winner of 6 advances to Playoffs

For group stage each game would last 9 rounds of Map 4 only

Playoffs -

Single match elimination/Knock out system.
The Game would be played as best of 3 map (9 rounds each of Map 1, 2, 3) in the playoffs.
